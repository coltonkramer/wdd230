Target Audience

The target audience of the site would be those looking for activities or information in the lower south eastern Idaho area. It could be anything from where can I buy bait to go fishing to how much does a gallon of milk cost at that one corner store. People can get information about events and sales in the area.

Personas

  • This person is planning a trip and is looking for a site that has infomation about the area
  • Another person has lived in Preston for 20 years but never gone to Soda Springs

Scenarios

  • Which stores have wheelchair access in town
  • How much does a rental cost in Bear Lake
  • What will the weather be on tuesday
  • How will I know where to go? I don't know the area.
  • How was this area established?
